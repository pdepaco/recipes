Chicken curry

This is a super easy and healthy recipe of chicken curry recipe. ou can combine it with some basmati rice. You can also swap the chicken for turkey, shrimp or tofu. You can swap the yogurt for cream or coconut cream. I hope it becomes one of your favorites as well!

Ingredients

  • 200g Boneless, skinless chicken breast skinless
  • 1 Garlic clove (or ground garlic)
  • 0,5g Curry powder
  • 50ml Plain Greek yogurt
  • 10g Extra virgin olive oil
  • 5g Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Remove excess moisture from the breasts using absorbent paper towels. paper towels.
  2. Cut the breasts into cubes and season with salt and pepper with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Finely chop the garlic and cilantro leaves.
  4. Place a griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the oil and oil and wait until it is very hot.
  5. Place the breast in the pan and cook, stirring continuously until the cubes are browned on all sides.
  6. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  7. Lower the heat and add some more AOEV to the same pan.
  8. Add the garlic, sprinkle the curry and cook stirring continuously for 30 seconds.
  9. Return the chicken to the pan and sauté for 1 minute more. Add more curry if necessary.
  10. Turn off the heat and add the yogurt, stirring until all the cubes are soaked all the cubes. If you like a more liquid sauce, add a little water (or milk) and let stand covered for 5 minutes before serving.
  11. Serve with cilantro leaves on top.
